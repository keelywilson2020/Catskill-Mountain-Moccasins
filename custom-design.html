Style Options

Natural Moccasins

These moccasins utilize a rougher, more ragged-edged leather for the tabs and layering on the uppers, creating a more "natural", woodsy or Road Warrior look.

Finished Moccasins

These moccasins feature clean, refined edges with smooth finishing for a more polished, contemporary look. This can also refer to shoes with design elements like welting, bead-work and layering of various trims and tabs.

    Sole Options

    We are proud to offer these 2 different types of soles to make your moccasins feel and wear exactly the way you want them to.

    Vibram Newflex

    The Vibram Newflex is our favorite sole, and the sole we prefer to use on our moccasins, unless there is some special need for an alternative. After 40 years of experimenting with different brands, tread patterns, and materials, the Vibram Newflex sole is the clear winner. It has great cushion, traction, flexibility, and is long-wearing. Vibram combined many of their soling technologies into this fantastic all-purpose, all-terrain sole. All Vibram soles are glued on to a rubber midsole, which is sewn on to the uppers. These soles can be replaced at any quality shoe repair facility. Optimized for comfort and durability, with the huge benefit of being endlessly resoleable, the Vibram Newflex sole is a $50 upgrade, but absolutely worth it.

    Conveyor Belt

    Our standard conveyor belt sole is made from polyurethane and has a normal wear expectancy of 5 years, but has been known to last 30 years or more, depending on the individual and wear pattern. This is the most tactile sole we use. It is thin and low profile, and feels very similar to a traditional leather sole, but with superior traction and durability. If you want more of a traditional moccasin feel, this is the sole you want.

    This type of sole can typically only be resoled once in the lifetime of the moccasin. As a specialty design, when the time comes for your moccasins to be resoled, you must send them back to us. There is no extra charge for this sole. Resoling costs $275.00 plus shipping.
